I'm not a therapist. Think of it more like a training partner for your life. Weekly calls, stuff to work on between them, and someone checking in mid-week who actually gets what you're going through because he's been there.

That's what the weekly calls and mid-week check-ins are for. I'll notice before you do. But if you're not willing to try, don't apply.

No. Most self-improvement starts from the idea that you're broken and need fixing, which is just the voice in your head winning. This is about understanding yourself. The changes come after that, almost on their own.
