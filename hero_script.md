# Hero Video Script (~2.5 min)

## CALL OUT — 15s

If you're watching this you probably already know something is off. You know you should be putting the phone down, getting to the gym, eating properly, being more honest with people. You know all of it. And you still can't make yourself do it.

## AGITATE — 25s

And I bet you've tried. You've watched the videos, tried the morning routines, maybe bought a journal or downloaded an app. And it worked for about a week and then you were right back where you started, except now you feel worse because you can't even fix yourself properly. That's because every single thing you've tried has been built on the same idea, that something is broken in you and you need to repair it. That's the voice in your head talking and it's been running the show since you were a kid.

## MY STORY — 25s

I know because I lived it. I grew up glued to my phone, gaming until 3am, watching stuff I'm not proud of, terrified of confrontation, honesty, women, the future. After my Masters in engineering I sent 350 job applications from my bed, 8 hours of screen time a day, vaping constantly. I looked like I had it together and I felt absolutely nothing.

## WHAT CHANGED — 30s

What actually changed wasn't a new routine. I started learning why I felt nothing. The neuroscience behind how stress and repressed emotions literally shut your body down. The ancient wisdom from the Stoics, from Tibetan philosophy, from Greek mythology, that's been saying the exact same thing for thousands of years. And the emotional work that ties it all together. Learning to hear that critical voice in your head and respond to it instead of obeying it. Learning to actually feel the things you've been running from your whole life and watching everything shift when you do.

## PROOF — 10s

I've made over 430 videos about this. 1.4 million views. And the messages I get every day from people saying this is the first time someone's put words to what they've been feeling is why I keep going.

## THE VISION — 15s

When you do this work, you stop forcing everything. You start enjoying your life because you're not fighting yourself anymore. You're more honest, more present, and the stuff that used to feel impossible just starts happening because you got out of your own way.

## OFFER — 20s

Everything I know is free across my content and always will be. If you want to go deeper, the Restoring Baseline community has 10 modules on emotional work, weekly live calls, and you can message me directly, and right now it's completely free. If you want to work with me one-on-one, I do 8 weeks of personalised coaching where we actually go into what's going on underneath.

## CTA — 10s

Have a look around the site. Everything you need is here. And if any of this resonated, I'd genuinely love to hear from you.
